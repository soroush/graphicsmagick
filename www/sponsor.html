Sponsor ImageMagick
ImageMagick has been available to both free and commercial users since the early 1990's. In early 1993 it was one of the strongest free image processing packages available, but, unlike competing packages, it continues to be supported and enhanced. Today, ImageMagick is stronger and better supported than ever.

ImageMagick has enjoyed considerable support from commercial users. Since ImageMagick is not restricted from use in commercial or proprietary applications, it is used to support thousands of commercial web sites, is used by professional digital image and publishing shops, and is used as a component of commercial applications. Many of these commercial users have made contributions to ImageMagick, large and small, often anonymous, but always appreciated.

ImageMagick Studio LLC is now looking for sponsorship from companies which use ImageMagick, or which would like to help support free software. If your company depends on ImageMagick, sponsoring ImageMagick development is a way to express your gratitude and to contribute back to the development ImageMagick.

ImageMagick sponsorship can take many forms. The following are some possibilities:

  • Provide assistance with legal matters related to ImageMagick Studio.
  • Support a high-bandwidth public ftp site to serve as the main distribution point for ImageMagick.
  • Provide technical writers to assist with the development of commercial-grade technical documentation.
  • Provide professional artists and web-site developers to help improve the ImageMagick web site.
  • Donate needed hardware and software in order help us develop a better product.
  • Donate source code to help improve ImageMagick where it is currently weak (such as support for ICC color profiles, or a GUI for a specific operating system).
  • Produce ImageMagick CD-ROMs for distribution.
  • Supply T-shirts, plush-toys, base-ball caps, and other collectibles to help promote ImageMagick.
